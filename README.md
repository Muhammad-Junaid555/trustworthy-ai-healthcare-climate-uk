# Developing Explainable, Fair and Scalable AI Algorithms for Healthcare Diagnostics and Climate Risk Prediction under the UK Trustworthy AI Framework

## Overview

This project focuses on developing trustworthy Artificial Intelligence (AI) systems for healthcare diagnostics and climate risk prediction. The research aligns with the UK's AI Opportunities Action Plan and Trustworthy AI principles, emphasizing transparency, fairness, explainability, accountability, and scalability.

The project aims to create machine learning models that can support decision-making in healthcare and environmental sustainability while maintaining ethical and regulatory compliance.

---

## Research Objectives

### Healthcare Diagnostics
- Disease prediction using machine learning
- Explainable AI for clinical decision support
- Bias detection and fairness evaluation
- Transparent risk assessment models

### Climate Risk Prediction
- Climate hazard forecasting
- Flood and drought prediction
- Environmental risk analysis
- Sustainable AI solutions for climate resilience

### Trustworthy AI
- Fairness evaluation
- Explainability techniques
- Responsible AI governance
- Human-centred AI systems
